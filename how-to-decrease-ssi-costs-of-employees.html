How to Decrease SSI Costs of Employees?

Some incentives determined within the framework of the law to reduce employee costs both provide a great advantage for employers and create another way of providing employment. In our country where unemployment rates are high, you can reduce the cost of your employees by using such incentives and contribute to the decrease in unemployment rates by providing employment.
