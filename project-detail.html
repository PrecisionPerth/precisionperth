Overview

Our aim is to provide the static projecting service of steel and reinforced concrete structures in accordance with the current specifications. Static project is the job of designing the most economical structure according to all unfavorable loads affecting a structure.

Detail

For a building, electrical, mechanical, architectural, ground survey and static projects must be done according to the current Zoning Law. Undoubtedly, all projects are very important, but the first and biggest factor affecting the cost of a steel structure is the weight of the steel structure. Therefore, the most important project in economic terms is the static project.
